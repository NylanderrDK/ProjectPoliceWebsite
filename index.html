About Project Police

What is Project Police?

Project Police is a multiplayer, police simulation game. With many features such as running backgrounds on ai, checking the inventory of a vehicle, arresting somebody, Project Police is a game with many things to explore. The game puts you in as a police officer in the world, and your "objective" is to patrol the streets, and keep the crime to a low. However, if you don't do your job properly you do not get punished in the game.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: When is the release date? A: We currently have no expected release date - but we promise you that we will keep working on it!


Q: What will the price be? A: We expect that the game will be released at Steam for $13.99.


Q: But what if i don't want to be a cop? A: Well there is a spot for you! We do intend on making a way for anyone to play as a civilian.
